One Piece

Law Trafalgar

Trafalgar D. Water Law, commonly known as Trafalgar Law, is a major character in One Piece. He is the captain of the Heart Pirates and a skilled doctor and swordsman. Law possesses the Ope Ope no Mi, a powerful Paramecia-type Devil Fruit that allows him to create a "Room" where he can manipulate objects, people, and even swap their body parts at will.
